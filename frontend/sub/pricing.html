Simple Pricing
I got a question: "Alex, how much does KRaft cost if I want to host a workload of my own? The answer is very simple; it is free*. KRaft has very low resource limits implemented on the virtual clusters, so you running a reasonable number of virtual clusters won't overwhelm my homelab resources. So that is the first catch: given KRaft is in beta right now, and my homelab is still fairly limited, you're free to host anything, within reason and within reasonable resource usage.
Maybe your second question is "but Alex, if it's free, then am I the product? And yes, yes you are, you are my test users. Your error traces and weird behaviour is the kind of data I steal from you muahahaha."
And finally, maybe you have one last question: "but Alex, I want to pay you for your amazing cloud platform". And firstly, wow, I am flattered. However, other than crypto addresses, I don't have payment setup. Instead, catch me at Cloud Native Mauritius events, and you can grab me a beer at our beer meetups.
